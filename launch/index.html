University of Alberta Engineering Program Visualizer

Welcome to the University of Alberta Engineering Progam Plan Visualizer.

This website is intended to help students and administration understand and visualize what courses they will take as part of their degree at the U of A.

To view a particular program plan, hover over one of the departments below. A dropdown menu containing the disciplines in that department will display.

Click on a discipline and you will be taken to the corresponding program plan page.
